Classic Hashbrowns

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons clarified butter
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch paprika

Directions

  1. Shred potatoes into a large bowl filled with cold water
  2. Stir until water is cloudly, drain, and cover potatoes again with fresh cold water
  3. Stir again to dissolve excess starch.
  4. Drain potatoes well pat dry with paper towels and squeeze out any excess moisture
  5. Heat clarified butter in a large non-stick pan over medium heat. Sprinke shredded potatoes into the hot butter and season with salt, black pepper amd paprika
  6. Cook potatoes until a brown crust forms on the bottom, about 5 minutes.
  7. Continue to cook and stir until potaoes are browned all over
  8. Serve
