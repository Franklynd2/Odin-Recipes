Classic Waffles

This is just a simple recipe to make some basic waffles. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm milk
  • 1/3 cups butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanillIa extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl mis together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar
  2. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs
  4. Stir in milk, butter and vanilla.
  5. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture
  6. Beat until blended
  7. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron
  8. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp
  9. Serve
